Privacy Policy for Nudge

Effective date: August 11, 2025

Thank you for using Nudge. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Information: You may provide your name, profile photo, bio, and LinkedIn URL.
  • Device & Bluetooth Data: We use Bluetooth to detect and connect with nearby peers. This process may collect device identifiers required for peer discovery but does not track your location.
  • Firebase Data: We use the Firebase SDK to support app functionality, such as crash reporting, analytics, and app stability. Firebase may collect anonymized usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Enable peer discovery via Bluetooth.
  • Allow you to share your profile with nearby users.
  • Improve app performance and stability through Firebase services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data is shared only as necessary to provide app functionality, including:

  • With Firebase (Google) for analytics and app stability monitoring.
  • As required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

  • You may choose not to enter personal profile information.
  • You can disable Bluetooth at any time, which will prevent peer discovery.

6. Children's Privacy

Nudge is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
